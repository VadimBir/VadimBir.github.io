<h1 style="margin-top:0;">About Me</h1>
<p style="margin-top:0;line-height:1.6">
I am a <b>PhD candidate</b> in the Department of Computer Science at the <b>Illinois Institute of Technology</b> working with <a href="http://www.cs.iit.edu/~scs/sun/biography.html">Prof. Xian-He Sun</a> at the <a href="https://grc.iit.edu/">Gnosis Research Center</a> on system memory architecture and memory hierarchy optimization. Concurrently, I serve as a <b>Computer Science Instructor</b> at the Department of Engineering, Computing, and Mathematical Sciences (ECaMS) at <b>Lewis University</b>, where I bring advanced research insights into the classroom to enhance student learning.
