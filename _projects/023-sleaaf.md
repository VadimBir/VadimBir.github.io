excerpt: "Designed and implemented the actor association subsystem for the SLEAAF automated assessment framework. Developed a spatial localization pipeline converting CNN-based face detection bounding boxes into calibrated physical room-space coordinates through geometric projection against known station geometry. A temporal backward-scan algorithm associates each sensor event with the nearest identified student via proximity to known item positions, with left/right/center disambiguation logic. Achieved **88% actor-event association accuracy** across five controlled experiments. *Published: ICCAD 2024. Supported by NSF Grant No. 2138846.*"
